Behavior Analyst

I went through a period of collecting degrees for fun! Joking aside, I just love learning. In a nutshell, Behavior analysis is a learning theory. Learning about learning, meta right?

The most interesting thing about pursuing this avenue is the personal growth I achieved. In addition to logical thinking, data analysis, and systematic decision-making, I learned the importance of behavioral flexibility (how to roll with those punches) and developed fluent compassion for everyone around me.

Climber

In my opinion, a little dirt on the skin and rock on your fingertips will do you good (although I’m not above pulling on plastic at the gym). I’m a free-spirit with a lot of ground to cover and most of my traveling revolves around touching real rock.

I love that climbing is like solving a puzzle with the tools you have (your body) and is an activity that allows me to continually set goals and challenge myself. Plus in the climbing community, you’re just surrounded by good people.
